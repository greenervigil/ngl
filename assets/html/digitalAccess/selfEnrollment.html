Self Enrollment

Get up and running quickly with the flexibility and control teachers desire for a smooth onboarding experience. Self Enrollment, via the NGLSync digital access portal, allows you to have complete control over your course management and student enrollment list.

Quickly and Easily Access Digital Programs

With self-enrollment, you as a teacher manage your own account, courses, and the course enrollment list. Students can self-enroll and create their own accounts making this a popular option for middle and high school students.

Easily Manage Digital Courses and Student Enrollments

As a teacher you will have full control and autonomy to create your courses and manage student enrollments. You can create courses and add or drop students at anytime.
