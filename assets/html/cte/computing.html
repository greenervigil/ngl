Computing and Information Technology


Getting Hands-on with Modern Technology
Computing and Information Technology titles are updated with current business technology, programming strategies and IT skills employers want. Computing and IT courses offer hands-on activities, simulation, or access to virtual machine labs that provide real-world practice with business software, programming languages, web development, PC Repair, Networking or Security.

Step-by-Step Instruction builds toward Mastery and Problem-Solving

Our titles offer clear instruction with visual examples that help students learn and practice the "how-to", while incorporating real-world scenarios that introduce the "why" and "what if?" critical thinking skills required for success in a variety of Business and IT careers.
