Precision Exams

Industry-Aligned Career and Technical Education Exams, used and loved by thousands.

Today, classrooms and budgets are bursting at the seams. Educators have to be more creative than ever to meet the needs of tomorrow’s leaders. Precision Exams can help! They provide high-quality, industry-aligned Career and Technical Education exams that help your students validate the knowledge and skills they have gained in the classroom. Also, educators receive powerful reporting tools they can use to evaluate their students’ understanding.

Precision Exams are stackable end-of-course and end-of-program capstone certifications that are backed by industry leaders and organizations like the National Consortium for Health Science Education, Business Professionals of America, Autoliv, Flowserve, and many more.
