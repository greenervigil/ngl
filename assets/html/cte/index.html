Career and Technical Education

Bringing Careers into the Classroom
National Geographic Learning, a part of Cengage publishes content written by authors who draw on experiences as CTE teachers and industry professionals. Our online solutions are developed by experienced Learning Designers who build interactive activities and assessments that engage students and prepare them for success in their careers. We are dedicated to helping students in their lifelong career journey, beginning in K–12.

The Skills Career Journey

Our titles offer digital learning tools that simulate work-place experiences, offer study and annotation tools and allow students to articulate what they are learning and share their relevant work with future employers.
