AP® Summer Institutes


Thank you!  Your request for materials for your AP® Summer Institute has been received.

If you have a Remote/Online Workshop:

  • eBooks: We will email you a link for you to forward to your participants so they can easily access the eBook prior to the workshop. Participants will have access to it for a full year. If there are multiple titles for a course or a Fast Track to a 5 AP® Test Preparation book, they will have access to all that is available.

  • Print books: Some participants may want a copy of a print book. This is always available to them by reaching out to their NGL Sales Consultant. For particular titles, we will also make this option available on the form that participants will fill out.

  • Access to Online Digital Platforms: Teachers may be interested in our online learning platforms such as MindTap or WebAssign. These platforms allow students to access the eBook, assessments, and other learning tools in one place, while teachers can customize their course and monitor students’ time-on-task and proficiency. We will allow all participants to gain access whether you request eBook or print books. In fact, teachers can go to NGL.Cengage.com/DigitalReview at any time to request access.

  • Participants: Please send us a participant list after the workshop.

If you have an In-Person Workshop:

National Geographic Learning will send you access to Cengage eBooks or ship to you print books for your participants. Here are a few things to know:

  • eBooks: We will email you a link for you to forward to your participants so they can easily access the eBook prior to the workshop. Participants will have access to it for a full year. If there are multiple titles for a course or a Fast Track to a 5 AP® Test Preparation book, they will have access to all that is available.

  • Print books: You may request up to 30 books for your participants if you will be in one location. Please only request what is needed, and email APSI.Support@cengage.com three weeks prior to the workshop to update us with quantities

  • Access to Online Digital Platforms: As students may continue with remote learning in the fall, teachers may be interested in our online learning platforms such as MindTap or WebAssign. These platforms allow students to access the eBook, assessments, and other learning tools in one place, while teachers can customize their course and monitor students’ time-on-task and proficiency. We will allow all participants to gain access whether you request eBook or print books. In fact, teachers can go to NGL.Cengage.com/DigitalReview at any time to request access.

  • Support: Email APSI.Support@cengage.com with any issues. That is the best way to reach us.

  • Participants: Please send us a participant list after the workshop.


Should you have any questions or changes, please email APSI.Support@cengage.com.


All the best in preparing for a successful AP® Summer Institute workshop!
