Pilot Terms
If the district chooses not to purchase materials at the end of the pilot, your National Geographic Learning/Cengage Sales Consultant will arrange for the return of the materials. At the end of the pilot period, if the district chooses not to purchase, access to digital programs will also be discontinued.

If the district chooses to purchase the program, a purchase order reflecting the pilot materials as well as any additional materials needed by the district will be submitted to National Geographic Learning. Please include the words NGLPILOT on the purchase order.

The educator of each pilot classroom is expected to complete and return an evaluation form; this will be shared with you, post pilot completion, by your National Geographic Learning/Cengage Sales Consultant.

Please ask your superintendent, business director, or other authorized district staff member to sign below and return the agreement to the address indicated.

Thank you for partnering with National Geographic Learning. I look forward to communicating with you regarding the success of your pilot.
